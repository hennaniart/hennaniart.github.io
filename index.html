✨ Meet Me

Hi, I'm Priya — born and raised in the vibrant city of Mumbai, India. My love for henna began at home, where I started applying henna on my mom’s hands. She adored it so much that no occasion felt complete without it. I was just 10 years old when I applied my very first design on her — and she loved it! Word quickly spread among family and friends, and soon I was everyone's go-to henna artist. My journey began right there. I even did my first bridal henna at just 14 for a close family friend.

Though my style has evolved over the years, my connection to henna and its roots has remained strong and meaningful.

After moving to the United States, I hadn’t planned on continuing my henna journey. But as fate would have it, in 2016, a friend’s daughter was getting married and had hired a professional henna artist. When their assistant couldn’t make it, I offered to help. What started as a spontaneous gesture turned into a turning point — everyone loved my designs and encouraged me to pursue it further. That moment reignited my passion, and I haven’t looked back since.

I truly love what I do. There’s nothing more satisfying than seeing the joy and smiles on my clients’ faces after their henna session. Each design I create is done with love, patience, and care — using only homemade, all-natural henna cones that I personally prepare for a safe and beautiful experience.

If you're looking to add a touch of henna magic to your life, I’d love to be part of your journey. Contact me today to schedule your appointment — and let’s bring your henna dreams to life.

Natural Henna Benefits

Natural henna is derived from the Lawsonia inermis plant and is safe for skin. It offers a cooling effect and is free of chemicals.

Henna Traditions

Henna is traditionally used for celebrations and ceremonies worldwide, including weddings, festivals, and religious holidays.

Why Choose Natural Henna?

Our natural henna paste is safe for all skin types, the process of creating henna designs can be a relaxing and therapeutic experience.

So whether you're looking to add some flair or connect with cultural traditions, natural henna is the perfect choice.
