Quick FAQ

What kind of henna do you use?
100% natural, freshly prepared henna paste. No black henna or chemical dyes. If you have allergies/sensitivities or are pregnant, please mention it in your message.
How long does bridal henna take?
Timing depends on coverage and detail. Share your inspiration + desired coverage and we’ll recommend the best time window.
Is a deposit required?
Yes. A deposit and signed contract are required to reserve your date and time.
Do you travel?
Yes — serving MD, VA & DC. Travel fees may apply depending on location.
