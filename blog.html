Henna Care & Safety (Quick Guide)

What is henna?

Henna (mehndi) is a natural plant-based dye from Lawsonia inermis. It fades naturally over 1–3 weeks.

Before care

  • Shave/wax 24–48 hours before
  • Avoid lotions/oils before application
  • Wear loose clothing

After care

  • Keep paste on 4–6 hours (overnight for darkest stain)
  • Scrape off with oil — avoid water 12–24 hours
  • Stain deepens over 24–48 hours

Henna used

100% natural, freshly prepared henna paste. No black henna or chemical dyes. If you have allergies/sensitivities or are pregnant, mention it in your booking message.
