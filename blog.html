Henna Before & After Care

Taking care of your henna is simple — good preparation and gentle aftercare make all the difference.

Before Your Henna Appointment

  • Shower before your appointment, as water should be avoided after application.
  • Avoid lotions, oils, or sunscreen on the area to be hennaed.
  • Henna takes about 30 minutes to dry, so plan to keep the area uncovered.
  • If you plan to get your nails done, do so before your henna appointment — nail oils and cleaning products can affect the stain.
  • Wear comfortable clothing, especially for longer sessions.
  • Choose outfits that allow easy access to hands, arms, feet, or legs.
  • Avoid clothing that may block the work area — sleeves, pants, or dupattas should not interfere with the design.

After Care (Golden Rule)

Just leave it alone.

  • Let the paste dry fully before moving the area.
  • Keep the paste on for several hours (or as long as possible) for a deeper stain.
  • Allow dried paste to flake off naturally; scrape gently if needed.
  • Do not use soap or water to remove the paste.
  • Avoid water for the first 24 hours for best stain development.
  • The stain will start orange and deepen to brown or mahogany over 24–48 hours.

Henna Used

100% natural, freshly prepared henna paste.
No black henna or chemical dyes.

Pregnancy & Sensitive Skin

Henna is safe during pregnancy when using natural henna paste. I have worked with many expecting clients and use only carefully selected, natural ingredients. If you have sensitive skin or allergies, please mention it during booking.
