All About Henna: Insights & Care

A simple guide for a rich stain and a smooth experience.

What is Henna?

Henna (mehndi) is a natural plant-based dye derived from Lawsonia inermis. It has been used for centuries to create beautiful temporary body art that fades naturally over 1–3 weeks.

Ingredients

Our henna paste is 100% natural and handmade using:

  • Rajasthani Henna Powder for deep, long-lasting color
  • Sugar to help the paste stick smoothly
  • Water to hydrate the powder into paste
  • Essential Oils (Eucalyptus/Lavender) for color release

Before Care

  • Shave or wax 24–48 hours before (let skin calm)
  • Avoid lotions/oils before application
  • Wear loose clothing to prevent smudging
  • Hydrate for healthier skin

After Care

  • Keep time: 4–6 hours minimum, overnight for darkest stain
  • Remove: oil + gently scrape off; avoid water 12–24 hours
  • Deep stain tip: clove smoke or a thin layer of Vicks after paste removal

Stain Progress & Safety

  • Starts orange → darkens over 24–48 hours
  • Usually lasts 7–14 days
  • We never use black henna
  • Please tell us about allergies/sensitive skin
