Tips, Care, and Henna Inspiration

What Is Henna?

Henna is a natural dye made from the leaves of the henna plant. For centuries, it's been used to adorn the skin with beautiful temporary designs during special celebrations and rituals.

Before & After Henna Care

For the best stain, avoid lotion before your henna session, and keep the paste on as long as possible. After removal, avoid water for 12–24 hours and moisturize daily to deepen the color.

How Long Does Henna Last?

Henna typically lasts 7–14 days depending on your skin type and how well you care for it. The design starts darkening 24 hours after paste removal, reaching its deepest stain in 2 days.
